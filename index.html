About WeatherAura

WeatherAura was born from the simple need for better, more reliable weather forecasts. As two tech enthusiasts with a passion for the outdoors, we wanted to create a weather app that not only serves accurate data but also connects users with the environment. This project is a culmination of our learning and experiences at Holberton School, and we're excited to share it with you.
